FAQ SECTION
Headline:
Frequently Asked Questions
Q: When will the Blueprxnt Circle launch?
A: The Blueprxnt Circle is currently in development. The Blueprxnt Base (free tier) and Collective tiers will launch in Q1 2026. Lab tiers will follow in Q2 2026. Practitioner Network and Mastermind are furthest out (Q3-Q4 2026).
Q: Can I switch tiers?
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Q: Is there a refund policy?
A: Monthly memberships can be canceled at any time (no refunds for partial months). Annual memberships are non-refundable but can be paused for up to 60 days per year.
Q: Can I join multiple tracks?
A: Yes! If you want access to both South Asian Health and Performance & Sport content, you can join both tracks at a discounted rate. Contact us for custom pricing.
Q: Do I need to be South Asian to join Track A?
A: No! While Track A is designed with South Asian physiology and culture in mind, anyone interested in culturally intelligent health strategies is welcome.
Q: I’m already a 1:1 coaching client. Should I join the Circle?
A: 1:1 coaching clients receive complimentary access to the Collective tier (Track A or B). You can upgrade to Lab or higher tiers at a discounted rate.
Q: How do I apply for the Mastermind?
A: Mastermind applications open once per year (Q4) for the following calendar year. Applications are reviewed by Pratik personally, and invitations are extended based on fit, commitment, and contribution to the community.

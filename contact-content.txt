Headline:
Quick Answers to Common Questions
Q: How quickly will I hear back after submitting the contact form?
A: We respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday). If you need a faster response, book a discovery call directly via our calendar link.
Q: Can I book a discovery call if I’m not sure which service is right for me?
A: Absolutely! Discovery calls are designed to help you explore options, ask questions, and determine the best fit for your goals and budget.
Q: Do you work with clients outside the United States?
A: Yes! Blueprxnt serves clients worldwide. All services are delivered virtually, and we work with international testing labs and partners to support global clients.
Q: How do I know if Blueprxnt is the right fit for me?
A: Book a discovery call or fill out the contact form with details about your goals and challenges. We’ll provide honest feedback on whether Blueprxnt is the right fit—and if not, we’ll point you in the right direction.
Q: Can I schedule a call outside of business hours?
A: Yes! Our calendar includes evening and weekend availability for international clients and busy professionals. If you don’t see a time that works, email us and we’ll find a solution.
Q: Do you offer payment plans for coaching and services?
A: Yes! Payment plans are available for Blueprxnt 1:1 coaching and select services. Discuss options during your discovery call or inquiry.
Q: I’m a member of the media. How do I request an interview with Pratik?
A: Email **speaking@blueprxnt.com** with details about your outlet, audience, and interview topic. We respond to media inquiries within 24-48 hours.
